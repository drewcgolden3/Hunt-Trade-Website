Our Story

Where luxury resale
meets community.

Hunt + Trade was born from Sree Lenhart's love of "the hunt" and great style. Her goal from day one: elevate the consignment experience by curating a trusted community of buyers and sellers right here in Hingham Square.

H+T's welcoming boutique environment offers great brands at great prices — and it's better for the planet. Stroll through Hingham Square, stop for a coffee, then drop off or discover something beautiful. That's the H+T experience.

Located at 60 South Street, Hunt + Trade has become a premier South Shore destination for high-end resale since 2019.
